Mahendra Singh Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer who captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from
2007 to 2016 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. Wikipedia
Born: 7 July 1981 (age 39 years), Ranchi
ODI debut (cap 158): 23 December 2004 v Bangladesh
Test debut (cap 251): 2 December 2005 v Sri Lanka
Last ODI: 9 July 2019 v New Zealand
T20I debut (cap 2): 1 December 2006 v South Africa
Last T20I: 27 February 2019 v Australia
